About The Hive

Honey Trap is a three-piece experimental rock band that creates sonic landscapes as intricate as a honeycomb and as sharp as a javelin's edge. Our debut album "You Never Hear The Javelin That Kills You" explores themes of unexpected beauty, hidden dangers, and the sweet poison of modern existence.

Drawing inspiration from the collective intelligence of bees and the precision of ancient warfare, we craft music that buzzes with energy while striking with surgical accuracy. Each track is a hexagonal chamber in our sonic hive, filled with golden melodies and venomous truths.

The album title reflects our philosophy: life's most profound moments often arrive unannounced, like a javelin cutting through silence or honey dripping from an unseen comb.
